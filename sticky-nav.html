Overview

FastSend is a fictional Kenyan payment platform designed to help people send money, manage records, and support small business transactions from one simple interface.

The goal is to make digital payments easier for students, traders, garage owners, restaurants, and service providers who need reliable transaction records.

Payments

Users can send money instantly, confirm successful transfers, and keep a simple record of who paid, when they paid, and why the payment was made.

This is useful for businesses that want to reduce manual paperwork and improve their daily cash management process.

Security

The platform uses verification steps before completing important transactions. This reduces mistakes and helps users feel confident when moving money.

Good security also protects customers from sharing sensitive details with the wrong person or sending money to incorrect accounts.

Business Growth

FastSend gives business owners simple reports that can help them understand sales activity, repeat customers, and payment trends.

With better records, a business can plan stock, manage expenses, and make informed decisions about future growth.
